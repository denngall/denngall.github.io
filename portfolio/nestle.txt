As a member of marketing burau at Nestle, mainly projects I participated were related with promotion of our brand “Rossiya” (chocolate bars).

Project #1. Loyalty program for the biggest Russian retail chains (more than 10.000 stores across the whole country). The main prize – tour for two persons for the most beautiful places in Russia. Were designed key-visual for our loyalty program, slogans, POSM, content of promotional packs, POSM testing, production planning.

Project #2. Special New Year format – two chocolate bars in one colorfully decorated package. More than 12.000 stores across Russia. Design of packages, SRP containers, production planning.

Project #3. Design of exclusive packages (3 chocolate bars in 1 pack) for national retailer.
