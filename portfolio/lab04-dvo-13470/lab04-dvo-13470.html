Justification for the new width for each device category

The most popular resolution for desktop and laptops is 768 or higher. This resolution is also popular with tablets (like iPad). Hence, for medium size tablets and computers with small resolution we could setup the width from 768px. The width for larger devices starts from 992px. The other category (up to 767) is for smartphones.
